AUDIO FILE NEEDED:

Place your startup chime audio file here as:
  startup-chime.mp3

Recommendations:
- Duration: 0.5-1.0 seconds
- Style: Minimal, elegant, executive-grade
- Avoid: Sci-fi sounds, harsh beeps, robotic tones
- Examples: Soft bell, gentle chime, premium notification tone
